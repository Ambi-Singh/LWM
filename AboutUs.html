ABOUT US



NIKKI SINGH : The Founder

NIKKI is the owner and manager of Learning World Montessori, also qualified as Level 4 Montessori teacher.

Having worked extensively with young children, toddlers and babies in various nurseries in London, Florence and Hyderabad (India), her 15 years of experience gives her immense understanding of different traditions, giving her a rare approach towards child education.

She founded her Montessori in 2017 and is extremely enthusiastic, passionate and caring while working with children. Her aspiration is to see every child reaching their maximum potential and grow into adults who are independent, responsible, and compassionate innovators.

OFSTED REVIEWS

Children are happy and settled in the childminder's care. Younger children seek comfort and enjoy lots of cuddles and support. Older children are becoming confident learners and keenly make choices during play. They know how to use the resources appropriately and are very well behaved. They show a good understanding of the daily routines. For example, they tidy away toys after use and line up to serve their own food at meal times.

Children enjoy lots of play-space and move freely between playrooms and outside. This enables them to actively follow their own interests during play and engage in activities that promote all areas of learning effectively. Older children are confident speakers, creating a vibrant environment where they talk enthusiastically with the childminder and assistants about what they are doing. Younger children are supported well in developing their communication and language through lots of songs and story times.

Children concentrate well during activities and persevere to complete their chosen tasks. They enjoy lots of praise from the childminder for their efforts and achievements. Children show secure bonds with the childminder and are kind to each other, building positive relationships and emotional security.
